SERVICES

Audio Restoration

As an audio restoration specialist, I offer professional services to clean, repair, and enhance audio recordings, ensuring the highest level of clarity and quality. Utilizing the industry-leading iZotope RX software, I can restore your audio, whether it’s music, podcasts, interviews, films, or archival recordings, bringing life back to damaged or noisy sound files. Whether you're dealing with degraded audio due to age, background noise from a poor recording environment, or digital errors such as clipping or distortion, my audio restoration service ensures pristine results.

Service Process

First I assess the audio file for noise, distortion, or other defects. Then using iZotope RX’s tools, I remove background noise, hum, clicks, and other unwanted elements. After that I fix issues like clipping, crackles, and reverb to restore clarity. In the end the restored audio is optimized for clarity, balance, and overall sound quality, delivering a professional-grade final product.

  • Noise Reduction
  • Click and Pop Removal
  • De-hum & De-buzz
  • Clip Repair
  • De-reverb
  • Spectral Editing

Audio Mixing

As a professional mix engineer, I offer high-quality audio mixing services that elevate your recordings to a polished, industry-standard level. Mixing is a crucial step in the music production process, where raw audio tracks are refined, balanced, and shaped into a cohesive final product that stands out both technically and creatively. I work across various genres, ensuring that each track is clear, dynamic, and aligned with the artist’s vision.

Service Process

I start by organizing and cleaning the session files. Then I proceed to adjust the levels and sculpture frequencies for clarity. After that I apply compression, reverb, and other effects to enhance the sound, making it sound more cohesive and full. Finally I refine the mix for clarity, balance, and dynamics, ensuring it’s ready for mastering.

  • Balance and Leveling
  • Modern Vocal Processing
  • Compression and Dynamics Control
  • Stereo Panning
  • Creative Effects Processing
  • Final Polish

Project Description

Welcome to my recreation of the iPhone 15 Pro webpage – an interactive and visually dynamic project designed to showcase my skills in React.js, GSAP, and Three.js. This project is more than just a webpage; it’s a fully immersive, cutting-edge experience that demonstrates the power of modern web development and animation techniques. By blending sleek, responsive design with advanced animations and 3D rendering, I’ve created a smooth, interactive showcase that brings the iPhone 15 Pro to life in the digital space.

The story

The inspiration behind this project was my passion for pushing the boundaries of web development. I’ve always been fascinated by how technology can enhance the user experience, and when Apple launched the iPhone 15 Pro, I saw an opportunity to merge my love for innovation with my technical expertise. Recreating the iPhone 15 Pro webpage wasn’t just about replicating a design – it was about capturing the feeling Apple evokes with their premium products. I wanted to create an immersive digital experience that feels just as sleek and cutting-edge as the product itself. From the high-definition visuals to the seamless user interactions, every part of the project was crafted to mirror the elegance of Apple’s design language, while showcasing my ability to integrate modern web technologies.

OUR APPROACH

To build this project, I employed a combination of React.js, GSAP, and Three.js to ensure a smooth and highly interactive user experience:
React.js provided the foundation for creating a fast and responsive interface, allowing for dynamic content updates without reloading the page. It was the perfect tool for managing the complex state and components that drive the functionality of the site.
GSAP was used to handle animations, offering smooth transitions, and scroll-based animations that keep users engaged as they navigate the page. It allowed me to create highly fluid animations that mimic the polished interactions you expect from a high-end brand like Apple.
Three.js was essential for incorporating 3D elements, like interactive models of the iPhone 15 Pro, adding depth and a wow factor to the webpage. This allowed users to interact with the product in a way that feels tangible and engaging, making the digital experience more immersive.

The story

As a sound engineer, I’ve always been curious about the choices made in the studio, and how elements like EQ, compression, and reverb can shape the feel of a song. So, I decided to recreate these songs, but with my own touch, focusing on the details that are often invisible to casual listeners. The album became a personal challenge: How could I bring something new to songs that have already been perfected? It wasn’t just about recording covers, but about making them my own through the mixing and mastering process. This project became a true labor of love, combining my passion for music with my expertise behind the console.

OUR APPROACH

Here's how I tackled the creation proccess of this album:
Mixing with Purpose: I took great care in balancing each element, ensuring that vocals, instruments, and effects all had their place. Each track was treated differently to suit its genre and vibe, with some receiving more aggressive treatments, while others embraced subtlety and warmth.
Mastering for Impact: In the mastering stage, I focused on achieving clarity and power. I enhanced the tracks for different listening environments, from headphones to high-end speakers.
Creative Reinterpretation: While the source material was faithful to the originals, I also added my own artistic touches. I used modern production techniques to elevate the sound, whether that meant adding atmospheric effects, reshaping the low end, or enhancing the sonic punch with compression. The result is an album that feels both familiar and fresh.

Project Description

More than just a tribute, it serves as a demonstration of my skills in mixing and mastering, where I bring a unique sonic texture to each track. Every song was chosen not only for its personal significance but also for the opportunity it provided to showcase my technical expertise and creativity in the studio. From goated Ye's classics, to the futuristic sounds of Daft Punk, the smooth flows of Drake, and the Latin flair of Bad Bunny, this album is a journey through sound, reimagined and remastered.

The story

The story of this collaboration began when I first heard MorganNoRarity's raw demos. It was clear from the start that this was an artist with something different to offer. Their music carried a raw, unfiltered energy that was both refreshing and challenging. Morgan wanted the album to reflect their journey—one filled with highs and lows, spontaneity, and moments of vulnerability. When I was brought on to mix and master the project, I knew that my job was to enhance that emotional core, without losing the edge that made MorganNoRarity’s sound so captivating. Throughout the process, we worked closely to ensure that the final mixes captured the right balance between the organic and the polished. Each song told a story, and my goal was to make sure the sound elevated that storytelling, while still feeling grounded in the artist's original vision.

OUR APPROACH

My approach to mixing and mastering MorganNoRarity's album was rooted in finding the perfect balance between clarity and character:
Mixing for Depth and Dynamics: I aimed to preserve the raw energy of the tracks while creating a sense of depth and space in the mix. This meant paying careful attention to the interplay of instruments and vocals, ensuring that each element had its moment to shine while never overpowering the other. I used EQ, compression, and reverb to create layers that gave the music a three-dimensional feel, helping MorganNoRarity’s voice cut through while still leaving room for the instruments to breathe.
Mastering for Impact: The mastering process focused on achieving maximum clarity and impact. I wanted the tracks to sound punchy, dynamic, and ready for any listening environment, from earbuds to high-end speakers. I focused on enhancing the low end for fullness, tightening the mids for warmth, and refining the highs for that final sparkle that makes the tracks pop. Throughout this stage, I was mindful of retaining the emotion and energy that MorganNoRarity had injected into every song.
Tailoring to the Artist’s Vision: I worked closely with MorganNoRarity to ensure that the sonic identity we created stayed true to the spirit of their music. We experimented with different effects and treatments to find the perfect balance between modern production techniques and the gritty, emotional rawness that defined the album. Every decision was made with the artist’s vision in mind, ensuring that the final product was a true reflection of who MorganNoRarity is as an artist.
